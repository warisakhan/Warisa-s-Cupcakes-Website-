About

Making the World Sweeter One Cupcake At A Time

Sisters Warisa and Paresha are co-founders of Warisa's Cupcakes which was established in 2020. Inspired by a childhood passion, these sisters have now created a great business basis in the Northern Virginia Area.

We pride ourselves in using fresh and organic products that are homemade, halal, kosher, and can be made allergy free to fit all of your accomodations.

We truly want to make an impact with our work in the world, so 20% of any order will be donated to any charity of the customer's choice. Our goal is to bake delicious treats while also donating to charities throughout the world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are Your Products Halal?
All products used are 100% halal and kosher including our vanilla and marshmallow products. We value utilizing organic and whole ingredients that can be enjoyed by everyone.

Can Cupcakes Be Delivered?
Cupcakes can be delivered to any locations within 15 miles of the Warisa's Cupcakes Home Base located in South Riding,Virginia. It is an extra $5 cost per every 5 miles. The minimum order for delivery is a dozen cupcakes.

Can Orders Be Made the Day Of?
Unfortunatley orders can not be requested for day of pick up. Orders must usually be placed at least a week in advance, but feel free to reach out 3-4 days in advance. Orders will be accepted based on availability.

Do I Need To Pre-Pay?
All orders must be paid for prior to pick up. Paying for the order in advance, secures your order spot and confirms your order has been sucessfully submitted.

How Do I Store Cupcakes Once I Receive Them?
We recommend storing cupcakes at room temperature and enjoying them the date of pick up. However,if cupcakes are being consumed 12 or more hours after pick up, we recommend refridgerating them to keep them fresh and moist.

Can I Order Seasonal Flavors on Off Seasons?
Orders can be placed for seasonal flavors in off seasons. However these flavors are based on availability and supply of products and are not always guranteed.
